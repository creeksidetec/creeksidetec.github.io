Cleanroom Gowning Procedures Class 1,000 thru 100,000
   


  1. Place both feet twice on tacky mat to remove debris from shoe soles.
  2. Dawn shoe covers making sure to cover entire shoe.
  3. Put on Bouffant cap and Beard cover (if necessary) making sure all hair is contained.
  4. Put on tyvek frock (Do not let frock contact floor).
  5. Dawn Clean-Room Gloves and Face mask (if necessary).
