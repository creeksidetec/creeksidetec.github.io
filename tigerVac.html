   CWR-8 Poly

  • Cleanroom Vacuum Cleaner System for wet & dry recovery
  • All stainless steel 304/316 for easy decontamination and excellent corrosion resistance.
  • With autoclavable recovery tanks and autoclavable floater system. Designed for applications where liquids and detergents are to be recovered in a controlled environment. Autoclavable floater system cuts off suction when recovered liquids reach capacity.
  • Static free and ESD safe. Our wet & dry vacuum cleaner are designed to be grounded during use. With electrical resistivity less than 10 ohms, any static produced while vacuuming is quickly and effectively discharged to ground.
  • EMI / RFI Shielded: Will not disrupt computers and sensitive electronic equipment.
  • ULPA filter efficiency of 99.999% on 0.12 micron. Dual ULPA filtration to filter both the working and cooling air. Each ULPA filter is individually laser tested to ensure Cleanroom compatibility and allows for maximum suction power.
