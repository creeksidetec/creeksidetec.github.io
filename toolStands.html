Tool Stands
   With the purchase of a precision electric screwdriver, you have made an investment in increased productivity. To protect your investment, it is recommended that the tool be hung on a tool support stand using either a tool balancer or positioner. Tool support stands help prevent the tool from falling off the work bench and will keep electrical cords up out of the operator's way. With the use of Velcro straps, any cords or attachments can be kept clear of the workstation.


   Tool Support Stands
   ASG offers a variety of tool support stands to assist in your requirements for workstation designs. These tool support stands vary from simple fixed units to flexible stands with complete adjustment in both the vertical and horizontal axes. The following tool stands are made of tempered anodized aluminum tubing. All fittings are engineered plastic with 20% glass reinforced polycarbonate for added strength and long life. Units have a rust-proof, chip-proof finish--no paint is used on any of the components.
  • Part 65006 is our most versatile tool stand. This unit has adjustments in both vertical and horizontal planes. Vertical adjustment is from a minimum height of 41" to a maximum height of 70". It is free to rotate a full 360 degrees. It can be locked in any position both in height or swing.
  • Height: 41" - 70"
  • Length: up to 3'
  • Part No. 65003 tool support stand is best suited for applications that require less vertical adjustment. Height is limited to the length of the main vertical arm. It will support one tool up to 15 Ibs.
  • Height: up to 36"
  • Length: up to 2'
  • Part 65025 *Discontinued* supports one tool up to 10 Ibs. Height is 46", maximum are is 2'4".
   Part Nos. 64312 & 64315 are ASG's simplest and least expensive tool stands. These units are intended to prevent tools from accidentally falling off a work bench. They should be used in conjunction with either of our tool positioners. Each of these stands has a workable height of 38". Part 64312 has an offset of 10" and Part 64315 has on offset of 18".
