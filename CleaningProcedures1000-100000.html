Cleanroom Cleaning Procedures Class 1,000 thru 100,000
   Daily
  • Wipe down all work surfaces with approved cleaner and CR wipes
  • Service Tacky mats
  • Replace Gowning stocks

   Weekly
  • Vacuum floors
  • Roll or wipe down walls
  • Wipe down Ceiling Grid and Tiles

   Monthly
  • Wash down Ceiling Walls and Floors with approved cleaner and CR wipes and mop
